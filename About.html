Company Overview

We are a professional building and civil contractor and service a variety of clients such as private individuals, developers, government, financial institutions and corporate accounts.

Our team consists of Construction managers, civil engineers, quantity surveyors, foremen and all relevant skills required to carry out any size of a building and civil construction contract.

Our Mission

To contribute to the economic development and the improvement of quality of life of the communities, by rendering efficient, effective and caring services and the creation of jobs and generation of skills through the community involvement and participation in worth while projects.
